Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide the audience with detailed information on the weather in the area where they live or a place they intend to visit.
That detailed information will include chances of precipitations, windchill, and temperature.
It will allow the visitors of the website to receive that useful information and plan their week accordingly.
