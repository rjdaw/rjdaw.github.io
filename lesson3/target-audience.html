Target Audience

Description

The target audience for this website is mainly adults who live in the area of Rexburg and want to know what kind of weather they can expect in order to plan outdoor activities.
Another important audience is foreign students who will arrive to Rexburg and don't really know what they can expect from the weather in this part of the globe, what clothes to bring.
Those two targeted types of audiences will be explored hereafter with the selected personas.

Scenarios

  • At what time does the sun rise and when does it set during the summer?
  • What will the level of water be like? Will it allow me to go kayaking?
  • What is the average temperature during the winter?
  • What kind of clothes are appropriate to wear during the different seasons?
  • How intense is the wind? Does it affect certain activities?
